## Features
- Play (almost) any song or video from YouTube Music
- Background playback
- Download audio chunks for offline playback
- Search for songs
- Add songs to your playlist
- Local playlist management
- Reorder songs in playlist or queue
- Dark/Dynamic theme
- Skip silence
- Audio normalization
- Persistent queue
-
